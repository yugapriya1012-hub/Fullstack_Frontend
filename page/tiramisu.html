Classic Tiramisu Cake

Ingredients

  • 1½ cups mascarpone cheese
  • 1 cup heavy cream
  • ½ cup sugar
  • 2 tsp vanilla extract
  • 1½ cups brewed espresso or strong coffee (cooled)
  • 2 tbsp coffee liqueur (optional)
  • 24-30 ladyfinger biscuits
  • 2 tbsp cocoa powder (for dusting)
  • Chocolate shavings (optional, for garnish)

A rich, creamy Italian dessert cake layered with espresso-soaked ladyfingers and mascarpone filling — indulgent and heavenly!

by emma's recipe

⏱ by 35 min.

Medium

Instructions

  1. In a bowl, whip the heavy cream and sugar until soft peaks form.
  2. Add mascarpone cheese and vanilla; beat until smooth and creamy.
  3. In a shallow dish, mix espresso and coffee liqueur.
  4. Dip ladyfingers quickly into the coffee mixture and arrange a layer in a pan.
  5. Spread half of the mascarpone mixture over the ladyfingers.
  6. Repeat with another layer of dipped ladyfingers and remaining cream mixture.
  7. Dust with cocoa powder and top with chocolate shavings.
  8. Refrigerate for at least 4 hours (preferably overnight) before serving.
