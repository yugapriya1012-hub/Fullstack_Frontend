Classic Chocolate Chip Cookies

Ingredients

  • 2¼ cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 tsp baking soda
  • ½ tsp salt
  • 1 cup butter (softened)
  • ¾ cup white sugar
  • ¾ cup brown sugar
  • 2 eggs
  • 2 tsp vanilla extract
  • 2 cups chocolate chips
  • 1 cup chopped nuts (optional)

Soft, chewy, and perfectly golden brown — these chocolate chip cookies are every baker's dream!

by emma's recipe

by 30 min.

Easy

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 350°F (175°C) and line baking trays with parchment paper.
  2. In a bowl, whisk together flour, baking soda, and salt.
  3. In another bowl, cream butter, white sugar, and brown sugar until fluffy.
  4. Add eggs and vanilla; beat until well combined.
  5. Gradually mix in dry ingredients until dough forms.
  6. Stir in chocolate chips and nuts (if using).
  7. Drop spoonfuls of dough onto trays and flatten slightly.
  8. Bake for 10-12 minutes or until golden brown.
  9. Cool on wire racks before serving.
