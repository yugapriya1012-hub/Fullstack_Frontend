Crispy Golden Waffles

Ingredients

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 2 tbsp sugar
  • 1 tbsp baking powder
  • ½ tsp salt
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1¾ cups milk
  • ½ cup melted butter
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • Optional toppings: honey, berries, whipped cream

Light, crispy, and fluffy waffles that are perfect for breakfast or dessert. Enjoy them warm with your favorite toppings!

by emma's recipe

⏱ by 25 min.

Easy

Instructions

  1. Preheat your waffle maker and lightly grease it with oil or butter.
  2. In a large bowl, whisk together flour, sugar, baking powder, and salt.
  3. In another bowl, beat eggs and mix with milk, melted butter, and vanilla.
  4. Pour wet ingredients into dry ingredients and stir until just combined.
  5. Pour batter into the preheated waffle maker and cook until golden brown.
  6. Serve warm with honey, maple syrup, or your favorite fruits.
